About This Product

The Sawor LED-Beam 120 is a compact moving-head built for live events, clubs, touring, and permanent installations. Its 2° beam, 7+1 color and gobo wheels, and 18-facet rotating prism deliver striking aerial effects and dynamic stage looks. Smooth dimming, strobe, and precise pan/tilt give full creative control via industry-standard DMX512. Lightweight and durable — equally at home on a truss or a touring rig. Supplied with power cable and mounting brackets.
