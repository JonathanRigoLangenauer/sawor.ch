About This Product

The Sawor CompactBeam punches well above its weight. At just 60W it delivers a peak intensity of 2,200,000 cd — comparable to industry-standard 180W beam fixtures, at a third of the power draw. Its small footprint and lightweight build make it easy to rig anywhere, from a packed touring case to a permanent truss installation. The 2° razor beam, 7+1 color and gobo wheels, and 18-facet rotating prism give you the full toolkit for aerial effects and dynamic stage looks. Smooth dimming, strobe, and precise pan/tilt keep you in full creative control via industry-standard DMX512. Supplied with power cable and mounting brackets.
