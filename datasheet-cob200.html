About This Product

The Sawor COB-Par delivers a powerful, uniform wash with exceptional color consistency across a tunable 3,200–6,500 K range and CRI ≥90. Smooth 0–100% dimming with four selectable curves, 1–20 Hz strobe, and DMX512/standalone operation make it equally capable in large venues and fixed installations. Die-cast aluminium housing, PowerCON in/out, and silent fan cooling keep it stage-ready night after night. Bright. Efficient. Dependable.
