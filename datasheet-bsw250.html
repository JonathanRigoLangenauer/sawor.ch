About This Product

The BSW 250 is a high-output moving head combining beam, spot, and wash in one compact fixture. CMY color mixing, a motorized 3°–35° zoom, dual prisms, 10 static and 7 rotating gobos, and linear frost give you complete creative range — from razor-sharp aerial beams to soft, even washes. 540°/270° pan/tilt with 16-bit resolution and automatic repositioning ensure smooth, precise movement. Bright. Flexible. Reliable. Built for stages, clubs, touring, and events where performance cannot be compromised.
